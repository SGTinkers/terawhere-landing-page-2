Why Terawhere?

Here are 4 simple reasons.

Ease of Travel

Tag along nearby drivers who are going for Terawih.

Punctual For Prayer

Take time on your iftar, and still make it on time for prayers.

Meet New People

Get to know other fellow Muslims while travelling.

Free To Use

The app will be free on both Android & iOS.

How To Terawhere?

Its as easy as 1, 2, 3!

As a Driver

Publish a Ride Offer

Provide pickup details, no. of seats available, and your Terawih destination.

Get Notified

You will be notified once a passenger books your ride offer.

Meetup & Move

Meet up with your new friends and bring them to Terawih!

As a Passenger

Book a Ride

Choose a ride, indicate the number of seats needed, and book it.
